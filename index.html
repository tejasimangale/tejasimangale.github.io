About

Tejasi Mangale is a current third year Computer Engineering student at Goa College of Engineering. She is an aspiring Front-end Developer with a mind full of curiosity, enthusiasm and a will to always learn something new. "Whatever you do, do it with perfection" is her mantra. She actively leads the GirlScript Fatorda City Chapter where their goal is to organize and promote the spread of tech through various initiatives and create opportunity for the youth via tech education. She along with her team have successfully managed to impact 200+ students through their initiatives and aims to inspire many more in her future ventures. She loves coming up with fun and innovative ideas and these inturn have served her success be it in school or college. She has been among the top three winners at "Hack to Help" Hackathon organised by IEEE Student Chapter and IIT Goa in association with GDG Goa.

Apart from coding and community work, she is passionate about sketching and trying other forms of art. In her free time she loves getting herself involved in reading books,making sweets and listening and grooving to music. She has an obsession of Greek mythology and is ever ready to have a chat over this!
